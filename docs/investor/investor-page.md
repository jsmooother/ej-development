# Executive Investment Narrative

## Investment Thesis

EJ Properties is executing a focused luxury residential development strategy in one of Europe’s most resilient premium real estate markets: the Costa del Sol.

The strategy is based on three structural trends:

1. **International lifestyle migration**
2. **Limited supply of prime hillside plots**
3. **Increasing demand for architecturally distinctive villas**

Urbanización El Madroñal in Benahavís represents a rare intersection of these dynamics.

The community offers privacy, security, large plots, and elevated views while remaining within short driving distance of Marbella, Puerto Banús, and the international amenities of the Costa del Sol.

Plots within this community are scarce and development density is intentionally restricted by planning regulations, creating long-term scarcity.

EJ Properties has secured a **3,037.85 m² residential plot** in this environment. The villa—**Villa Elysia**—is dimensioned at approximately **757 m² AMES closed built** (sales methodology), with detailed schedules totaling **~803 m² enclosed**; see the live investor portal “Site & planning” section for floor breakdowns and reconciliation.

The project is designed by **AMES Arquitectos**, a Marbella-based architectural firm with significant experience in luxury residential development.

The development objective is to create a **distinctive architectural villa tailored to the international luxury buyer segment**, which has shown strong demand in the Marbella region over the past decade.

The Marbella region has experienced sustained growth in luxury residential demand driven by:

International buyers represent a significant share of the high-end market and frequently target:

This supply constraint creates a favorable environment for boutique developments with strong design differentiation.

# 1. Project Overview

EJ Properties is developing a luxury villa project located in **Urbanización El Madroñal**, a gated hillside residential community in **Benahavís**, one of the most prestigious municipalities in the Marbella region on the Costa del Sol.

The development strategy focuses on acquiring premium plots in low-density gated communities and developing architecturally distinctive villas targeted toward international high-net-worth buyers.

This project involves the development of a **high-end contemporary villa** designed by **AMES Arquitectos**, an established architectural firm with extensive experience in luxury residential design in Marbella.

The completed property will be marketed to international buyers seeking:

- privacy
- views
- proximity to Marbella
- secure gated living

The Costa del Sol has become one of the most attractive luxury real estate markets in Europe, driven by:

- international lifestyle migration
- favorable climate
- strong tourism infrastructure
- expanding international airport connectivity
- increasing demand for luxury villas

Benahavís is a municipality located between Marbella and Estepona and is widely recognized for:

- exclusive gated communities
- low-density zoning
- strong property values
- strict urban planning regulations

The municipality contains several high-end residential areas including:

- La Zagaleta
- El Madroñal
- Los Flamingos
- Monte Mayor

El Madroñal is a **private gated estate** located above San Pedro de Alcántara.

- forest environment
- panoramic sea and mountain views
- private security
- low-density villa zoning
- large plots

The community is particularly popular with international buyers from:

- Northern Europe

- staff apartment and technical core

- courtyard and vertical light shaft  
- gym, office, man’s cave  

First floor:

- master suite  
- guest suites with en-suites  
- terraces and porch

infinity pool, gazebo, porches and terraces, landscaped garden
