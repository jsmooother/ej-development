## Top 10 Risks

1. **Topography & retaining scope** – Geotech + structural concept; staged earthworks
2. **Vegetation/tree constraints** – Arborist survey; landscape strategy
3. **Permit duration uncertainty** – Pre-consultation; compliance matrix
4. **Utilities feasibility** – Utility letters early
5. **Spec creep (luxury)** – Design freeze gates; change control
6. **Cost escalation** – Long-lead locks; index tracking
7. **Contractor performance** – Prequal; performance security
8. **Market pricing miss** – Comp pack; differentiated product
9. **Financing availability** – Multiple funding tracks
10. **Handover/occupation friction** – Documentation early
